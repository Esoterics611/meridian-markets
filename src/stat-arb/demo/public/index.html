Validate before you trade — robustness tools

⚠ Parameter sweep and Monte-Carlo still run on the engine's synthetic feed — they show the machinery's shape, not numbers for a live pair. The two real OOS buttons are the gate: they run the active scanned/backtested pair over real Binance history, re-fit β per train slice, judge it out-of-sample net of fee + spread + impact, then discount the Sharpe for selection bias (Deflated Sharpe over the # of pairs your last scan ranked) and flag thin history (regime coverage). Read the Verdict: PASS needs Deflated Sharpe ≥ 0.95. No strategy ships on in-sample numbers.
Flow: ⊹ Scan → click a pair's ⚖ (sets the active pair) → run an OOS gate here. Walk-forward = sequential train→test windows (shows β drift). Purged k-fold = interior folds with purge+embargo (leakage-robust). Both honor the top-strip Lots/leg.

② ⚖ Size & ③ ▶ Trade

Fees are 5 bps of notional × 4 fills (~20 bps round-trip) — a fixed percentage, so net edge in bps is the same at any lot size; bigger lots only multiply the dollars (and risk), not the edge. The real cap is market impact (∝ N²) — the impact-optimal lots N* below is the most you can deploy before impact eats half the edge.
