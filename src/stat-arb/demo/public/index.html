Investor Disclosures

No customer capital is in this system. Phase 3 operates against a synthetic price feed and a mock execution venue. All real venues (RealBinanceVenue, RealOndoYieldProvider) are dormant and refuse to fire until KYB closes and a feature flag is flipped under written sign-off.
This is not an offering. Track-record numbers shown are from a synthetic-feed backtest — see the stat-arb course for the underlying math. Past simulated performance is not indicative of future returns.
Customer onboarding gates at Phase 4 behind a 3(c)(7) fund wrapper. Until then, the only capital at risk in adjacent Lira-Bridge is Path C first-party reserve float, governed by the ITreasuryClient contract — not investor money.
